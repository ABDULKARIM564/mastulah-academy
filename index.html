About the Academy

Mastulah Theology Online Academy is dedicated to nurturing students in authentic Islamic knowledge and moral excellence. Our mission is to make high-quality Islamic education accessible to learners worldwide, guided by the Qur’an and the Sunnah. We believe in the power of knowledge to transform hearts and uplift communities.

Courses Offered

Qur’an Recitation & Tajweed

Learn proper recitation and pronunciation of the Qur’an following traditional rules of Tajweed.

Islamic Theology (Aqidah)

Understand the foundations of Islamic belief and strengthen your spiritual understanding.

Arabic Language

Build fluency in reading, writing, and speaking Arabic — the language of the Qur’an.

Fiqh (Islamic Jurisprudence)

Study Islamic rulings and learn how to apply them in daily life.
